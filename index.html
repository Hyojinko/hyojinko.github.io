About Me

Hyojin Ko is a Researcher of medical artificial intelligence at the Asan Institute of Life Sciences/Asan Medical Center. Her research interests include artificial intelligence, image analysis, deep learning, knowledge graph, and large language models. At her current position, she focuses on deep learningâ€“based medical image analysis integrated with large language models, aiming to develop clinical decision support systems (CDSS) that support automated medical image interpretation and structured clinical report generation. Her work emphasizes end-to-end pipelines that connect image understanding with language-based reasoning to assist clinicians in real-world diagnostic workflows. During her master's research, she studied multimodal language models that jointly process images and textual data. In parallel, she researched on explainable AI (XAI)-based CDSS, incorporating knowledge graphs to enhance model interpretability, clinical reasoning, and structured knowledge representation.
